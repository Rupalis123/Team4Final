Correlation Matrix

Explanation

To observe any correlation within and between the categories of pre-existing conditions and symptoms, we use matplotlib and seaborn to create a correlation matrix for each feature. Higher correlation is noted in patient populations who had underlying illnesses of COPD/lung disease and diabetes or COPD/lung disease and chronic kidney disease. Within the hypertension column, patients mostly had shortness of breath followed by fatigue as a symptom. Fever and cough symptoms usually presented together followed by cough and diarrhea. By using correlation matrixes with data for a specific hospital patient demographic, this can be helpful for clinicians to identify trends and patterns seen in Covid19 patients.
